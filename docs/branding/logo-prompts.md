# PuzzleSolver — 10 Logo Design Prompts

Ready-to-paste text-to-image prompts for the **PuzzleSolver** brand (puzzlesolver.space).
Each is a complete, self-contained prompt plus its palette, best use, and a negative prompt.

## Brand palette (use these exact hexes)
- Canvas (warm off-white): `#faf9f6`
- Ink (deep navy): `#1a1a2e`
- Primary rose: `#f43f5e`
- Accents: blue `#3b82f6` · amber `#f59e0b` · teal `#14b8a6` · purple `#a855f7`

## Recommended generators & settings
- **Vector logos + any lettering:** Recraft (SVG output) or Ideogram (best text rendering).
- **Painterly/icon concepts:** Midjourney v6 (`--ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6`), DALL·E 3.
- For icons/favicons use a **1:1** ratio; for header lockups use **3:1** or **16:9**.
- Append this **style suffix** to any prompt: *"flat vector, clean geometric shapes, crisp edges, scalable, plain background, professional logo"*.
- Append this **global negative**: *"no jigsaw puzzle pieces, no gradients (unless specified), no drop shadows, no 3D bevels, no photorealism, no glossy reflections, no garbled text, no clutter"*.

> Recommended starting point: **#1 Sun/Moon Cell** (single iconic glyph, brand-defining, favicon-perfect) and **#2 PS Interlock Board** (timeless monogram). Generate those first.

## 1. Sun/Moon Cell  ⭐ recommended
**Concept:** A single rounded-square board cell split diagonally into a sun and a crescent moon — the Tango duality — as one confident puzzle glyph.
**Best for:** favicon · app icon
**Palette:** `#f43f5e` · `#1a1a2e` · `#faf9f6` · white bg

> Design a flat vector logo mark for a logic-puzzle brand: a single rounded-square 'board cell' (generous 22% corner radius, like an app tile) divided along a soft diagonal into two halves. The upper-left half holds a simple filled sun glyph — a solid circle with six short stubby triangular rays — in primary rose #f43f5e; the lower-right half holds a clean crescent moon (a circle subtracted by an offset circle) in deep ink navy #1a1a2e. The two halves nest together with a thin 2px gap of warm off-white #faf9f6 running between them so the diagonal reads as a crisp seam, like two pieces of a Tango puzzle locking in place. The cell sits on a plain pure white background, perfectly centered with even margins. Geometric, minimal, friendly-but-smart, high contrast, legible at 16px favicon size; even line weights, no gradients, no shadows, no text, flat 2D vector illustration, sharp scalable shapes.

**Negative:** no text, no gradients, no shadows, no 3D, no realistic sun/moon rendering, no jigsaw pieces, no glow, not cluttered.

## 2. PS Interlock Board  ⭐ recommended
**Concept:** A rounded-square board whose grid lines carve a continuous 'P' and mirrored 'S' from negative space, interlocking like a solved path.
**Best for:** favicon · app icon
**Palette:** `#faf9f6` · `#1a1a2e` · `#f43f5e`

> Design a minimalist geometric monogram logo for a logic-puzzle brand, built strictly on a 4x4 grid. The mark is a single rounded square 'board' tile with a 14px corner radius, rendered as a solid warm off-white shape (#faf9f6) sitting on a deep ink field (#1a1a2e). Inside the board, carve a letter 'P' and a letter 'S' out of negative space so the ink shows through as thin, even-width channels exactly aligned to the grid lines; the stem of the P and the spine of the S share one vertical channel so the two letters interlock into one continuous form, like a solved puzzle path. Single uniform stroke weight, square modules with subtly rounded ends, golden-ratio proportions, crisp pixel-grid alignment. Two colors only: ink #1a1a2e and off-white #faf9f6, with the P accented in rose #f43f5e where it crosses the center channel. Flat vector, no gradients, no shadows, no texture, no bevels. Centered with even padding, symmetrical bounding box, favicon-first to stay legible at 16px. Plain white background, SVG-ready geometry.

**Negative:** no jigsaw clichés, no gradients, no drop shadows, no 3D, no realism, no extra text, no serif type, no flourishes.
